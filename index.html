Assalamu Alaikum Everyone

We hope you're all having a blessed Ramadan. We'd like to invite you to join us for a special Iftar gathering on Tuesday, 10th March. This gathering will be for brothers only, and the meal will be completely free.

In the spirit of Ramadan, let's come together as one Ummah. This is more than just a meal — it's an opportunity to strengthen the bonds of brotherhood, to build connections within our Oldham community, and to share in the blessings of this sacred month together. Whether you're new to the area or have been here for years, you are warmly welcome.
